Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 16, 2026

You have the right to delete your Artic.le account and all personal data associated with it at any time. This page explains how.

Quick summary The fastest way: open the app, go to Profile → Account → Delete Account, and confirm. Your account and all personal data are deleted immediately. If you no longer have access to the app, email diogodow@gmail.com and we'll complete the deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your email address and display name
  • Your password hash (if applicable)
  • The identifier linking your account to Google or Facebook, if you used social login
  • Your list of followed sources
  • Your tagged (saved) articles
  • Your reading preferences (theme, text size, default summary lens)
  • Any cached AI summaries associated with your account
  • Your crash reports and analytics events tied to your account identifier

What is retained

After account deletion, we may retain the following for a limited period:

  • Anonymous aggregate usage statistics that cannot be linked back to you.
  • Backup copies that are automatically purged within 90 days.
  • Records we are legally required to keep under Brazilian law (for example, transaction records if you made a paid purchase). These are minimized to what the law requires and are not used for any other purpose.

How to delete your account

Option 1: From within the app (recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data is directly from the app:

  1. Open Artic.le and tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
  2. Tap Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice in the dialog that appears.

Your account and personal data are deleted immediately upon confirmation. You will be signed out and the app will return to the welcome screen. This action cannot be undone.

Option 2: Email us

If you no longer have access to the app (for example, you uninstalled it or lost access to your login method), you can request deletion by email:

Send an email to diogodow@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: Account deletion request
  • The email address associated with your Artic.le account (must match the sender's address for verification)
  • Optional: reason for deletion (this helps us improve the app, but is not required)

We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Option 3: Revoke access from Google or Facebook

If you signed up using Google or Facebook login and simply want to disconnect your account from that provider, you can do so from their account settings:

  • Google: myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Facebook: Settings → Apps and Websites → Active → Artic.le → Remove

Revoking access from the provider will disconnect your login method but does not automatically delete your Artic.le account data. To fully delete your data, please also use Option 1 (in-app) or Option 2 (email).

Timeline

  • In-app deletion (Option 1): Your account and personal data are deleted immediately upon confirmation. You will be signed out right away.
  • Email request (Option 2): We confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days. This timeline aligns with LGPD Art. 18 and Facebook Platform Policy requirements.
  • Backups: Even after immediate deletion, data may persist in automated encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being fully purged. These backups are not used for any purpose and are not accessible to us outside of a disaster-recovery scenario.

Questions or problems

If you have trouble with the deletion process, or if you do not receive a response within the timelines above, please contact us at diogodow@gmail.com. If you believe your data rights have been violated, you may also file a complaint with the Brazilian National Data Protection Authority (ANPD) at gov.br/anpd.
